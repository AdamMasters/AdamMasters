# Hi, I'm Adam Masters 👋
 
A recipient of accolades such as [Apple Distinguished Educator](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1DmDgHtpL9HGOAwwrMfcQ6NOd1QJhcknj/view?usp=sharing) and [Microsoft Innovative Educator Expert](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1s3qA6x5FL_9n8fZMDmyIbbP5cWRffMQA/view?usp=sharing) since 2015, Adam's academic journey includes a BSc in IT from Salford University, postgraduate studies in E-Commerce at [Aberdeen University](https://www.abdn.ac.uk/ncs/departments/computing-science/), and an MSc in Educational Leadership & Management at [Manchester Metropolitan](https://www2.mmu.ac.uk/). His dissertation, focusing on the integration of new technologies in secondary school curricula, highlights his commitment to forward-thinking educational strategies.
 
Adam's professional history is marked by significant achievements, including the development of outstanding KS3 Computing, GCSE CS, and IB CS curricula at [Jumeirah English Speaking School](https://www.jess.sch.ae/). His tenure as Head of Computing at [Deira International School](https://www.disdubai.ae/home) is notable for his implementation of Microsoft OneNote and Teams, revolutionising the school's digital learning approach. This innovation contributed to the school's 'outstanding' rating and its recognition by the International Schools Award (ISC) in 2021 for Use of Digital Technology in the classroom.
 
Beyond his contributions to curriculum development and digital innovation, Adam is a respected voice in the educational technology community. His expertise extends to training and mentoring educators, advocating for sustainable technology integration, and leading initiatives like the establishment of a paperless Computing department. His presentations at major educational conferences and exhibitions in the UAE, including talks on the future of learning, demonstrate his influence and thought leadership in the field.
 
Adam's profile is a testament to his unwavering commitment to enriching the educational landscape. His journey reflects a blend of innovative practice, leadership, and a deep understanding of the evolving needs of 21st-century learners. As he continues to inspire and shape the world of education, his story serves as a beacon for educators striving to integrate technology and pedagogy in meaningful ways.
